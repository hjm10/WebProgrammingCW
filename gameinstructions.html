Welcome to out Monster Game

This is a game about running away from a monster whilst avoiding hitting the pillars in front of you by passing through the gaps in between them. We hope you enjoy the game.

Instructions:

1. Use the arrow keys to move around the screen

2. You get a 10 secind head start on the monster chasing after you, make it count!

3. Avoid the pillars in front of you to prevent the monster from catching up to you by passing through the gaps in the pillars.

4. If you hit the pillar the monster will start to catch up to you.

5. If the monster catches up to you the game will be over.

6. You can play the game again if you do not win against the monster on your first try.

ENJOY!!
